What is the structure of your dataset?¶

The main dataset contains 113,937 rows and 81 columns. With regards to the datatype, a huge percentage of the columns in the dataset are either integer or float in nature.

However, with the new dataset (df) created, a greater percentage of the datatypes are categorical in nature. For example,

  • credit_grade
  • prosper_rating
  • borrower_state
  • employment_status
  • loan_origination_quarter

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

The feature that have undoubtedly pique my interest is the borrower_rate variable. I am interested to know the relationship between these selected variables and the borrower_rate

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

I envisaged that prosper_rating as well as credit_grade would influence the borrower_rate: the higher the prosper_rating or credit_grade, the lower the interest rate. Also, the term (tenor) of the loan would influence the borrower_rate: the longer the tenor, the higher the interest rate.
